Meet Riza

Riza+Rachel Catering serves delicious food and is here to serve you and your guests. Whatever your palate desires, we offer dishes that can be tailored to fit any budget; for example, stuffed mushrooms can be filled with a homey mixture of bread crumbs, cheese and bacon or we can create a splurge-worthy crab-stuffed portobello mushroom.

We accommodate a variety of health, diet and lifestyle needs; kosher, vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, pescatarians and those with allergies.

Many of our dishes serve as jumping-off points for inspiration. If you are throwing a Mexican fiesta, we can do phyllo triangles, paninis, pizzas and soup shots with the appropriate ingredients. The creative possibilities are endless when you choose Riza+Rachel Catering.
